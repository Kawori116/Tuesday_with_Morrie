"We all need teachers in our lives."

"Tuesdays with Morrie", Morrie shares his reflections on the art of letting go - of people, possessions, and ultimately, life itself. Through his wisdom and guidance, we learn that acceptance and surrender can be the keys to finding peace and fulfillment in the face of life's many challenges.

Don't let go too soon, but don't hold on too long.

Mitch Albom

Lessons on Love

    Every Tuesday, they met,
    A dying man and a friend.
    Together they talked and wept,
    Sharing lessons until the end.

    Welcome to my website dedicated to the book "Tuesdays with Morrie" by Mitch Albom. Our goal is to honor the legacy of Morrie Schwartz and share the valuable life lessons he imparted to his student, Mitch Albom, with the world. Through this website, we hope to inspire and encourage readers to live more meaningful and fulfilling lives.
